Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email, business/store name, phone number, and payment details. We also collect usage data such as conversation logs with our AI agent and broadcast order history, used to operate and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including your AI agent configuration and dashboard.
  • To process payments and verify manual payment submissions (OMT, Bank Transfer, COD).
  • To review and process broadcast orders, including the contact lists you submit.
  • To communicate with you about your account, orders, and support requests.

3. Contact Lists & Broadcast Data

When you submit a contact list for the Broadcast Service, you are responsible for ensuring that data was lawfully collected and that you have a legitimate basis to contact those individuals or businesses commercially. We review submitted lists as part of our order approval process but do not independently verify the lawful basis of every contact's data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party providers to operate the Service, including Anthropic (for AI processing via Claude), Meta/WhatsApp Business API (for messaging), and payment processing partners. These providers process data only as necessary to deliver the Service.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active, and broadcast order records for accounting and compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us via your dashboard or the About page to make a request.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the Service or email where appropriate.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out via our About page.
