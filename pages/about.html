Who We Are

TableBot is an AI-powered WhatsApp commerce platform built specifically for restaurants and retail merchants across the MENA region. We help businesses turn WhatsApp — the channel their customers already use every day — into a reliable sales, support, and marketing engine.

We started TableBot because we kept seeing the same problem: restaurant teams drowning in reservation messages, menu questions, and complaints — all while WhatsApp sat there as an underused, untapped channel capable of handling all of it automatically.

What We Do

Our platform combines a Claude-powered AI agent with reservation handling, menu delivery, and complaint management — all delivered through WhatsApp, the most-used messaging app in the region. We also operate a WhatsApp Broadcast Service, helping restaurants reach precisely segmented B2B and B2C audiences.
