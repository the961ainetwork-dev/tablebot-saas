This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs all use of the TableBot Broadcast Service, including any mailing list provided by TableBot ("house lists") or submitted by you ("customer lists"). By submitting a broadcast order, you agree to comply fully with this policy.

The Broadcast Service may be used exclusively for legitimate commercial business communication. Examples of permitted content include:

Product launches, promotions, and sales announcements
Order, shipping, and service updates
Event invitations (commercial, corporate, or retail events)
B2B partnership, pricing, or catalog communications
Loyalty programs and customer retention messaging

3. Prohibited Use

You may not use the Broadcast Service, any house list, or any customer-submitted list for:

Content that violates any individual's privacy or publicity rights
Harassment, hate speech, discriminatory, or defamatory content
Misleading, fraudulent, or deceptive commercial claims
